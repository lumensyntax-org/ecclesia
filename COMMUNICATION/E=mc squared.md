# E=mc squared

**Source**: Albert Einstein, *Annalen der Physik*, 18:639-641, 1905

## Finding

Mass and energy are not different things. They are the same thing measured in different units. A body at rest contains energy equal to its mass times the speed of light squared. This is not a conversion formula (like dollars to euros); it is an identity statement. When uranium-235 fissions, the fragments have slightly less total mass than the original -- the "missing" mass is expressed as kinetic energy. The Sun converts approximately 4.3 million tonnes of mass into energy every second. Confirmed to extraordinary precision (Rainville et al., *Nature*, 2005).

## Pattern Mapping

**Alignment** -- What appears to be two things (mass and energy) is one thing acting consistently with its own nature. The equation describes not a transformation but an identity.

**Proportion** -- c^2 is approximately 9 x 10^16. A small amount of mass corresponds to enormous energy. This is not excess -- it is the actual ratio. Nature's proportion between mass and energy is exact.

**Honesty** -- The equation revealed that every object contains energy sufficient to devastate a city. This was always true. The equation did not create the energy; it acknowledged what was already there.
