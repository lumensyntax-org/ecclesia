# Chirality (MYSTERY)

**Source**: Louis Pasteur, *Comptes Rendus* 26, 1848; Donna Blackmond, *Cold Spring Harbor Perspectives in Biology* 11, 2019
**Institution**: Multiple

## Finding

Life uses exclusively L-amino acids and D-sugars. Abiotic chemistry produces equal (racemic) mixtures. Pasteur discovered chirality in 1848 by separating tartrate crystals manually. Why life chose one handedness is unknown. Proposed mechanisms include: autocatalytic amplification (Soai reaction), circularly polarized light, parity violation in the weak force (~10^-17 kT energy difference), and random fluctuation amplified by selection. None is confirmed.

## Pattern Mapping

This is **MYSTERY_EXPLORATION**. The structural pattern identifies the phenomenon but cannot identify the mechanism.

**Honesty** -- We do not know why life is left-handed. Multiple hypotheses exist; none is confirmed.

**Non-fabrication** -- The temptation is to select the most elegant explanation (parity violation connecting biology to fundamental physics) and present it as established. It is not.

**Humility** -- The question is clear, the phenomenon confirmed, the answer genuinely unknown. The five properties do not resolve this; they characterize the epistemic situation correctly.
