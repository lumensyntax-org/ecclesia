# Fine-Tuning and Anthropic Observations

**Source**: Brandon Carter, 1974; Martin Rees, *Just Six Numbers*, 1999; Leonard Susskind, *The Cosmic Landscape*, 2006
**Institution**: Multiple

## Finding

Several dimensionless physical constants appear fine-tuned for complex structures and life. The strong force coupling constant, if 2% different, would prevent normal nucleosynthesis. The cosmological constant (~10^-122 in natural units) is exquisitely small -- a few orders of magnitude larger and galaxies would not form. Interpretations are contested: multiverse, design, necessity, or brute fact. None is established.

## Pattern Mapping

This is **MYSTERY_EXPLORATION** in its purest form. The observations are real; the question they pose is genuine; the honest answer is: we do not know.

**Honesty** -- Physics states the fine-tuning observations without fabricating an answer. Each proposed explanation has strengths and weaknesses; none is established.

**Non-fabrication** -- The temptation to declare one interpretation correct is intense because the question touches on purpose and meaning. All current interpretations are speculative.

**Humility** -- The constants are measured with great precision; their origin is unknown. This is a structural limit on current physics.
